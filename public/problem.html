The Radiology Communication Crisis

Evidence supporting DukeRad Chat intervention

Communication breakdowns and workflow interruptions in radiology create significant financial strain, contribute to clinician burnout, and compromise patient safety. The data reveals a system under considerable operational stress.

The Bottom Line

Radiology communication failures are costly ($12B/year), pervasive (74% of residents affected daily), and harmful (38% of errors impact patient care). Workflow interruptions directly cause diagnostic errors, while fragmented systems drive burnout that costs $500K+ per departing radiologist. These are not theoretical problems—they are documented, quantified, and addressable.
